## About This Book

The rise of AI-powered search represents a fundamental transformation of how information is sought, processed, and delivered. By 2028, Gartner predicts that brands' organic search traffic will decrease by 50% or more due to generative AI-powered search.

This guide shares insights from extensive analysis and monitoring of how various AI tools behave, exploring the future of SEO and GEO (Generative Engine Optimization). You'll learn:

- **How AI search actually works** - from query processing to response synthesis
- **Where LLMs get their knowledge** - training data sources and web retrieval
- **Content optimization strategies** - making your content AI-friendly
- **Technical SEO for AI** - JSON-LD, robots.txt, and indexing for AI crawlers
- **The future of search** - preparing for an AI-first world

## Who This Book Is For

This guide is specifically tailored for:
- **Digital Marketers** who need to adapt their strategies for AI visibility
- **Content Strategists** creating content that AI can understand and cite
- **SEO Specialists** implementing technical optimizations for AI crawlers
- **Business Owners** who want to understand how AI search affects their brand

## What Makes This Guide Different

Unlike generic AI guides, this book:
- Focuses on **actionable strategies** with practical implementation checklists
- Explains the **"why" behind the tactics** by connecting AI mechanics to SEO strategies
- Covers **AI-native platforms** (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity) not just AI-enhanced traditional search
- Includes **real examples and case studies** from actual AI search behavior
